About RapidRings

The affordable health monitor

RapidRings is an affordable health ring that tracks and monitors your heart rate, intercepts emergencies before they happen, and keeps an active tracker on the location of the wearer. RapidRings is based in Tokyo, Japan where we manufacture, distribute, and advertise our product. Our target market is primarily middle aged Japanese individuals with elderly parents that they wish they had more time to take care of. Our mission is to help our target consumer get more time back in their life while also protecting the ones they love. We are currently the only affordable luxury health ring on the market and we take pride in this.

Our company executives is composed of five people. Heath W., President and Location Leader, Nicholas K., Vice President and Chief Marketing Officer, Nick B., Chief Financial Officer, Daria I., Chief Operations Manager, McKayla E., Human Resources Manager. Together, we strive to help strengthen and protect the Japanese population by providing health monitors to allow for everyone to live care free knowing they are protected from future danger.

Below is the description of our three major pillars. Our major pillars are things we promise to accomplish with our products and strive to meet everyday as a company. The three are our dedication to track a healthy heart, our ability to intercept emergencies and keep your loved ones safe, and our ability to track the location of the wearer to keep loved ones safe.

Pillar 1

Dedication to tracking a healthy heart.

We promise to track the heart rate of the wearer and report honest information. This promise extends to saving important data and being able to allow you to share it with your doctor.
Pillar 2

Dedication to predicting and intercepting emergencies.

We promise to save information over time to be able to help predict and analyze heart patterns. Here we hope to be able to predict emergencies given irregular patterns being detected.
Pillar 3

Dedication to tracking location of the ring.

We promise to track the location of the ring to be able to locate wearer. This ensures the wearer is safe and loved ones are made aware.
RapidRing V1

RapidRing V1 is the first and currently only product sold by us. This ring comes with the standard three pillar features we promise. There is also customization for color, initial engravings, and personal sizing. The ring also will come with the app for free. In order to recieve the benefits the ring will give, the app must be used. The app will guide you to input your health and demographics to better suit your personal style.
